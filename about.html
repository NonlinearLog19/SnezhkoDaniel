More About Me

My name is Daniel Snezhko, and I am an 18 year old photographer and videographer. I was born and raised in Mount Vernon, WA.

Growing up, I remeber my dad would always carry his camera with him. He loved photography. On my 14th birthday, I got my first camera and Ever since, photography and videography have been my hobbies. Other things I like to do in my free time is hiking, motorcycle riding, and hanging out with my friends.

I got my first job at 16 by word of mouth. I never made any posts or offered anyone my services, but it's thanks to my friends I started this side hustle.. My current work is detailing at a recreation products dealership. Currently I am attending Skagit Valley College, pursuing my associate's degree in web design.
